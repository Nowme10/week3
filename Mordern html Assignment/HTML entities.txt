HTML Entities
ans: HTML character entities are used as a replacement of reserved characters in HTML,
these characters are replaced because some characters are reserved in HTML.
if you use less than (<) or greater than (>) symbols in your text, the browser can mix them with tags that's why character entities are used in HTML to display reserved characters.
Examples:
1)© : copyright (&copy)
2)& : ampersand (&amp)
3)® : registered treadmark (&reg)
4)<  : less than (&it;)
5) > : greater than (&gt;)
